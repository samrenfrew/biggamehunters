About the Guild

We all enjoy the spoils of raiding, who doesn't like receiving loot for their efforts? But we try to take influence from The Great Game Hunter himself; we make the most of our raid experience, whether that's banter between friends, or endlessly reminding the shaman about that time he popped Heroism at an inopportune moment (seriously Ben, what were you thinking!?)

We have a strong core raiding team, many of whom have been playing WoW since the 'good old days' of Vanilla. We raid twice a week in the evenings in order to keep a moderate progression pace balanced with real life. We focus on remaining 'Ahead of the Curve', but have also moved into Mythic content as we bolstered our roster numbers in Legion.
