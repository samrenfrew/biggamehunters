Current Roster

Hemet Nesingwary – Guild Master

Considered the greatest hunter on Azeroth, he leads his guild on hunts through Azeroth and beyond.

Gamekeepers – Officer

Very few are chosen as Hemet's elite Gamekeepers. These individuals go above and beyond what is expected by a Big Game Hunter, often organising hunts for others, or poaching new Gun Caddys to bolster the ranks where the weak have fallen.

Big Game Hunters – Veteran

Big Game Hunters are the veterans, the seasoned, the elite killing machines within this self titled guild. They are undisputed in their hunt prowess and often reminisce about the time 'we slew that dragon' or 'felled that old god'. Newly fledged hunters look up to them for advice for where best to stand for the kill (spoilers: not in the fire), or how to avoid being mind controlled and killing everyone. They have proved that they have the competence and commitment to hold this rank within the guild.

Gun Caddy – Member

The majority of Gun Caddys cannot tell you the difference between a Naaru and a Gnoll. Some choose the life of a Gun Caddy, whereas others find themselves unable to progress to the prestigious 'Big Game Hunter' title. Those new to the guild will have to prove their worth on a hunt in order to progress past this rank.
